About me...

I’m a displaced West Country bumpkin living in Leeds who’s hoping the PhD I’m currently enrolled on will allow me to say with certainty that I’m a ‘former’ geography teacher. I’m a closet crafter, avid reader and owner of the cutest cava-shell named Bear.

I enjoy travelling (but then who doesn’t?) and while I can’t do the month-long tours I was able to as a teacher I hope my 4 years as a research student are peppered with weekend breaks to interesting European cities.

The Assignment 1 page outlines the requirements of the 30% portion of assessment for GEOG5995M, and details the programming that went into the agent based model that was generated throughout the lecture series.

The Something Extra is an authentic Sri Lankan pineapple curry recipe that is quick, easy and full of flavour. I add it not because it’s relevant, it isn’t… at all, but rather because it’s delicious and everyone should try it.
